ChannelDeck

A focused macOS IPTV player for Xtream-style accounts, with live categories, fast channel browsing, full-screen playback, and local credential storage.

Available now

Built for live IPTV playback

Xtream-style login

Connect with server URL, ID, password, and the provider's player API.

Live categories

Loads live categories and channel lists, with search and category filtering.

Favorites

Save preferred live channels locally and browse them from a dedicated category.

Pinned channels

Keep important channels in an ordered top-shelf category that persists locally.

Persistent recents

Recently played channels stay available after relaunch and can be cleared.

Now and next guide

Fetches short EPG data for the playing channel when the provider exposes it.

Multiview playback

Watch two to four channels side by side with per-tile volume and mute controls.

Local recording

Record authorized streams locally to the Mac and reveal saved files in Finder.

Local library

Open, reveal, refresh, and delete local recordings or saved playlists from one place.

M3U export

Save a local playlist to the ChannelDeck library for compatible players.

Playback diagnostics

Shows player status, stream host, format, and copyable error summaries without credentials.

Secure local storage

Stores passwords in the macOS Keychain and keeps public builds credential-free.

Roadmap

  • iOS and iPadOS apps with shared account and playback model.
  • Real recorded demo for the website and README.
  • Developer ID signing, notarization, and a cleaner macOS installer flow.
  • Full EPG grid and richer schedule browsing.
  • Richer saved-channel management for favorites, pins, recording metadata, and history.
  • VOD and series support from compatible provider APIs.
  • Picture-in-picture and improved external player handoff.
  • M3U playlist import where legally supported.

Content and credentials

ChannelDeck is a client only. It does not include IPTV content, subscriptions, provider credentials, playlists, or authorization to access streams. Local recordings and playlist exports are intended only for streams you are authorized to use.
